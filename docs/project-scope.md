# Project Scope

TalkSpark focuses on one job: helping AI assistants produce better Chinese talking-head short-video scripts.

## In Scope

- Spoken Chinese scripts for creators.
- First-second hooks.
- Concrete scene building.
- Hot-topic and entertainment commentary with fact-checking boundaries.
- Ad and submission brief compliance.
- Duration estimation.
- Quality review for teacher tone, marketing tone, vague claims, and factual risk.

## Out of Scope

- Full video editing.
- Auto-posting to social platforms.
- Scraping private platform data.
- Writing fake personal experiences.
- Making unsourced claims about real people or companies.
- Replacing editorial review for high-risk public claims.

## Design Tradeoff

TalkSpark prefers clear maintainable references over a single giant prompt. This makes the skill easier to audit, adapt, and improve.
