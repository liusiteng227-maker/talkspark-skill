# Fact Check

Use this reference when a script mentions real people, companies, products, recent events, public disputes, financial claims, lawsuits, rankings, dates, or quotes.

The goal is not to turn every口播 into a report. The goal is to keep the spoken script emotionally alive while avoiding factual mistakes.

## When To Verify

Verify before writing if the request includes:

- A real person, founder, celebrity, executive, creator, or public figure.
- A company, brand, product, platform, model, or organization.
- A recent or changing event.
- Claims about money, valuation, financing, lawsuits, sales, market share, rankings, dates, appointments, resignations, or product launches.
- A quote, slogan, interview line, or alleged statement.
- Anything that could damage a real person's or company's reputation if wrong.

## Source Preference

Prefer:

- Official company pages, official announcements, filings, court documents, exchange disclosures, and verified social accounts.
- Reputable media reports with dates and named sources.
- Direct interviews or event transcripts.

Use caution with:

- Unsourced social media posts.
- Reposted screenshots.
- Commentary videos.
- SEO content farms.
- Anonymous claims.

## Minimum Check

For fact-sensitive scripts:

1. Identify the factual anchors.
2. Check at least 2 reliable sources for public claims when possible.
3. Note dates. Current facts may change.
4. Keep only facts that are necessary for the口播.
5. Convert uncertain claims into softer wording.

Example:

```text
Hard claim, "X一定会成功。"
Safer口播, "X能不能成我不知道, 但他现在这个状态很有意思。"
```

## Fact vs Opinion

Separate three layers:

- Verified fact, what happened, who said what, when, where.
- User opinion, what the speaker thinks or feels.
- Inference, what the speaker reads from the situation.

Good:

```text
俞浩是追觅科技创始人, 这是一层事实。至于他到底能不能把那些大想法都做成, 我不知道。但我想为他说的是另一件事。
```

Bad:

```text
俞浩一定会成功, 因为他代表了未来。
```

## If Browsing Is Unavailable

Say briefly:

```text
我没有实时核实外部信息, 下面只基于你给的信息写观点口播。
```

Then avoid:

- New dates.
- New numbers.
- Unverified allegations.
- "最新", "刚刚", "已经证实", "官方确认" unless the user supplied a source.

## Spoken Output With Sources

Usually do not put source citations inside the spoken script. It breaks口播 flow.

After the script, optionally add:

```text
事实核实，已核实X、Y；未核实Z，稿中已做模糊处理。
```

If the user asks for sources, provide them outside the口播 body.
